Broccoli Stroganoff

Description

A delicious vegetarian broccoli stroganoff. It`s fast, easy to make and really tasty!

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of sliced broccoli
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 pint sour cream
  • 8 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced

Steps

  1. In a large skillet add garlic powder, salt and pepper and stir in for a little over medium heat. Add broccoli and stir in for 5 minutes.
  2. Add onion and mushrooms to skillet and saute until onions are translucent. Remove from heat (very important) and add sour cream. Stir all together and serve.
